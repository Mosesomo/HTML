Recipe for wet fried Tilapia fish

Ingredients

  1. 2 Tomatoes
  2. 1 onion
  3. cooking oil
  4. Garlic
  5. Coriander
  6. 1/2 cup water

cooking instruction

  1. step 1:Put cooking oil in a preheated sufuria
  2. step 2:Add onions and cook until light brown
  3. step 3:Add garlic and cook for half a minute
  4. step 4:Add water and bring the mixture to boil
  5. step 5:Under low heat deep the fishand cover to cook for 5 mins
  6. step 6:sprinkle Coriander on top and set aside
  7. step 7:Serve hot withc veges and ugali
